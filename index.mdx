Speculate turns trading into a competitive e-sport. Enter a trading competition, trade a simulated balance head-to-head against other players, climb the leaderboard, and win real USDC prizes — while stacking XP toward the **\$SPEC** token.

<Note>
  **The trading is simulated — the stakes are real.** You trade a **\$10,000** practice balance on Speculate's own engine, priced live off Kraken market data. Entry fees and prize pools are real USDC, and your maximum loss in a paid competition is capped at your entry fee.
</Note>
