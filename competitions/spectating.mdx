Almost any competition on Speculate can be watched live. You don't need an account, a wallet, or a single dollar of USDC to pull up a lobby and see the action unfold. Spectating is **open to logged-out visitors**.

As a spectator you get the same real-time view competitors see, minus one thing:

- **The live leaderboard** — every trader's rank and live ROI
- **The price chart** — same feed the competitors are trading against
- **The order book**

The one thing you don't get is the order-entry form. Watching is free; trading isn't — a prompt invites you to join the competition whenever you're ready to put a position on.

Once a competition ends, spectators see the same final results screen as everyone else: settled rankings and the winners who cleared the payout curve. See [Payouts](/competitions/payouts) for how that curve works.

<Tip>
  Spectating is one of the fastest ways to learn Speculate before you risk an entry fee. Watch the live leaderboard, the price action, and how ROI swings across a lobby, then join a free competition to try it yourself.
</Tip>
