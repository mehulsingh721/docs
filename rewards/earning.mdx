XP is earned by competing, not by showing up. Every dollar you put on the line, every streak you keep alive, every rank you climb — it all converts into Season XP. This page is the complete reference.

<Note>
  XP only ever comes from **competitions** and **social** activity. [Duels](/duels/rules) are winner-take-all cash matches and award **no Season XP**.
</Note>

## Trading & competitions

Your buy-in is the foundation. Everything else — streaks, placement, avoiding liquidation — stacks on top of it.

| Action | XP |
| --- | --- |
| Enter a competition | **1 XP per \$1** of entry fee |
| Daily participation bonus (buy-in of **\$10 or more**) | **\+25 XP** |
| Duels | **0 XP** — duels never award Season XP |

<Tip>
  A $50 buy-in earns **50 XP** on its own. Clear the $10 threshold on the same day and you pick up the **\+25 XP** daily bonus automatically — no separate action required.
</Tip>

### Streak milestones

Qualify for the daily participation bonus on consecutive days and you'll unlock one-time streak bonuses at these thresholds:

| Streak | Bonus (one-time) |
| --- | --- |
| 3 days | **\+150 XP** |
| 7 days | **\+600 XP** |
| 30 days | **\+4,500 XP** |
| 90 days | **\+25,000 XP** |

Each milestone pays out once per streak — break the chain and you start climbing back toward the next one from zero.

### Placement bonuses

Where you finish adds a **percentage on top of your buy-in XP** — not on your winnings, on the XP your entry fee already earned.

| Placement | Bonus |
| --- | --- |
| 1st place | **\+60%** |
| 2nd place | **\+25%** |
| 3rd place | **\+15%** |
| Top 10% | **\+10%** |
| Top 25% | **\+5%** |
| Avoided liquidation | **\+10%** |

The liquidation bonus is independent of rank — survive the competition without getting liquidated and it applies regardless of where you place.

## Worked example

A **\$50 buy-in**, finishing **2nd**, and surviving the competition without liquidation:

| Component | XP |
| --- | --- |
| Buy-in (1 XP × \$50) | 50 |
| Daily participation bonus (buy-in ≥ \$10) | \+25 |
| 2nd place bonus (25% of buy-in XP) | \+12.5 |
| Avoided liquidation (10% of buy-in XP) | \+5 |
| **Total** | **92.5 XP** |

## Social XP

Growing the Speculate name pays too. Link your accounts, follow, and post — each converts into XP.

| Action | XP |
| --- | --- |
| Link your Discord | **\+100** (one-time) |
| Link your X account | **\+100** (one-time) |
| Follow [@SpeculateTrade](https://x.com/SpeculateTrade) on X (verified) | **\+1,000** (one-time) |

### Posting on X

XP for a qualifying post scales with your follower count — the bigger your reach, the bigger the payout.

| Followers | XP per post |
| --- | --- |
| 100 | 1 |
| 1,000 | 50 |
| 5,000 | 110 |
| 10,000 | 250 |
| 50,000 (super-post) | **1,000** |
| 500,000 (super-post) | **2,500** |

<Note>
  These are the current Season's **default values** and can be tuned per Season. Check [XP & Seasons](/rewards/xp) for how a Season is structured, and [Tiers](/rewards/tiers) for what your total XP unlocks.
</Note>
